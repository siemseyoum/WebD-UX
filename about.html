ABOUT

This site is here to support students across Manchester who are studying at Manchester Metropolitan University. To have a one stop website for all the necessary help or assistance that they could need during their time studying with us. The topics are broken into three categories

Wellbeing:

for support with health, mental health or counselling related.

Social:

that helps with breaking down walls to build friendships with social events, sports clubs or sobrieties.

Other:

which helps with the finance, accommodation or technical issues that happen during assignments.

We want to promote the types of support and help to make everyone on our campus aware that we are here to support you in any way we can, to make sure you succeed whilst studying at MMU and get the best experience possible.
