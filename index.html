Student Support

Here at Manchester Metropolitan University, we provide a variety of support for both on campus face-to-face help and online support for off campus enquires. All to enable you to achieve your best possible experience while studying here and give you the necessary assistance if you need.

At MMU we want all our students to achieve their full potential whilst studying with us and also to enjoy the overall university experience as this is a special time in people’s lives. We understand that it can be challenging and stressful time in young people’s lives with leaving home, studying at a higher level or external factors away from the campus.

The University has created this website to assist you to find any help or support for your given situation might require. If it’s about accommodation we can help finding the right place for you with our residence support. Looking to make you friendships? We got you covered with our social events schedule. Need hardware/tools for those technical assignments? That can be found in our equipment hire section.
