Student Visa

To study in the UK, first you must apply for a student visa at least 6 months before you start the course. Here is a link to the official gov site, where you can check eligibility and apply for a student: Student Visa Application

Student Visa Overview

  • Must be over 16 to apply for a visa
  • Must be accepted to a licensed university course (must show evidence of this)
  • Must be able to support yourself when you move and to pay for the course
  • Must be able to speak, read, write and understand English
  • Must have parental consent if you are under the age of 18 (must show evidence of this)

    MMU International Scholarships and Funding

    There are four different types of scholarships are offered to international students in MMU:

  • General and Chancellor's international awards: awarded to exceptional international students who enrol on a full-time taught course. You must apply for this scholarship.
  • Postgraduate scholarships for specific courses: The University's five faculties are also able to provide scholarships to international students studying selected postgraduate courses. (click here for more info)
  • Research grants: Some research projects at Manchester Metropolitan are able to offer scholarships to postgraduate students who work on them. (click here for more info)
  • Sports scholarships: We provide financial help for leading sportspeople to study at Manchester Metropolitan in the UK. (click here for more info)
